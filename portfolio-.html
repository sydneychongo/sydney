More about me

Hello, I am Sydney Chongo

Web Developer and Web Designer

I am Sydney Chongo, but you can just call me Sydney. I was born and raised in Zambia, and over the past decade, I have been building my skills and expertise in the fields of web development, graphic design, and branding strategy.

In my work, I aim to produce elegant solutions that surprise and delight users, while also considering the practical considerations of implementation, scalability, and maintaining a manageable workload for developers.

Throughout my career, I have had the opportunity to work with teams, ranging from small, nimble groups working on experimental projects to larger development teams with more structured product lifecycles. In these roles, I have taken on a range of responsibilities, including designing user interfaces and user experiences, developing front-end code, leading growth efforts, managing projects, and more.

In addition to my day-to-day responsibilities, I enjoy taking on learning and exploring different technologies that can be used to get work done easily while achieving all my goals and those of the customers.

If you have an interesting project that you think I might be able to help with, I would love to have a conversation and learn more about it.

Work flow

My workflow to make offer my service

Step 1

Project request

The first step in our collaboration is the project request where you fill the project request form to give me and idea of what you are looking for.This helps me determine if the the right person for your challenge

Step 2

First Meeting

building trust is one important thing i like to do before working together.In this meeting ,we get to know more about each other which helps me determine if am the right person for your project.We also get to talk about your goals and requirements,clarify questions and decide whether to proceed to the next step.

Step 3

Proposal

After I have learned as much as possible about your project, I will create an individual proposal. I don't charge for my work by the hour but on a per-project basis. This way, you'll have a complete overview of the costs upfront.

Step 4

Implementation

Once I have everything needed, I'm ready to start with the implementation. Ensuring transparent communication and regular updates is a priority for me. I'll keep you in the loop using convenient communication channels like whatsapp.

Step 5

Launch

Before launching, the finalized website will go through quality assurance to ensure every detail is upto to the standard agreed upon or better. Depending on the project, I'll help to launch via code export.

Step 6

Performance analysis

After launching, the website will go through quality monitoring to ensure everything works well.
