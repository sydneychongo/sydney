About Me

More about me

Hello, I am Sydney Chongo

Graphic Designer and Web Designer

My name is Sydney Chongo, but you can just call me Sydney. I was born and raised in Zambia, and over the past decade, I have been building my skills and expertise in the fields of web development, graphic design, and branding strategy. These areas involve creating and improving products for the internet, mobile devices, and computers, with a focus on both aesthetics and technical capabilities.

In my work, I aim to produce elegant solutions that surprise and delight users, while also considering the practical considerations of implementation, scalability, and maintaining a manageable workload for developers.

Throughout my career, I have had the opportunity to work with a variety of teams, ranging from small, nimble groups working on experimental projects to larger development teams with more structured product lifecycles. In these roles, I have taken on a range of responsibilities, including designing user interfaces and user experiences, developing front-end code, leading growth efforts, managing projects, and more.

In addition to my day-to-day responsibilities, I enjoy taking on learning and exploring different technologies that can be used to get work done easily while achieving all my goals and those of the customers.
